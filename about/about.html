About the Texas Rugby Referee Association

Who we are • What we do • How to get involved

Mission

The Texas Rugby Referee Association (TRRA) recruits, trains, coaches, assigns, and supports match officials across Texas. We develop referees at every level, promote safety and game management grounded in World Rugby Laws, and help raise the standard of rugby in our region.

What We Do

  • Education & Certification: Support USA Rugby pathways for accreditation, compliance, and ongoing development.
  • Appointments: Assign referees and assistant referees for league, collegiate, club, and tournament play statewide.
  • Coaching & Feedback: Provide on-field coaching, evaluations, and development plans.
  • Community: Foster a supportive culture for officials and collaborate with competitions and clubs.

Governance & Partnerships

  • Bylaws: The TRRA Bylaws (approved 2014) define membership, elections, and operations.
  • Texas Rugby Union (TRU): TRRA works alongside the TRU, which oversees adult rugby in the region and sets related policies.
  • USA Rugby: Referee accreditation, SafeSport, background checks, and Law guidance align with USA Rugby and World Rugby.

View TRRA Bylaws (PDF)

Coverage

TRRA supports community, collegiate, and club rugby throughout Texas (and, when requested, nearby competitions). Assignments span regular season fixtures, playoffs, and tournaments.

Get Involved

New to refereeing or returning to the whistle? Start with USA Rugby registration and required certifications, then connect with TRRA for local mentoring, coaching, and appointments.
